Email Instructions

Please order by email.

Cooler prices include shipping. Sales tax will be added.

Send an Email indicating which Cooler(s) you want to purchase. We will email a QuickBooks Invoice that you can pay by Credit Card, PayPal or Venmo. Once you make your payment the Coolers will be packed with dry ice and ship on Mondays or Tuesdays. We will email your tracking number once the cooler ships.

Our coolers ship by FedEx ground from a frozen storage warehouse in Canton, Missouri. Please include a shipping address that FedEx can deliver to. We cannot ship by any other method.

Please include your name and phone number so that we can contact you if necessary.
