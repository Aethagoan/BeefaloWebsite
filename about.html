All About Us!

We are fourth generation farmers/ranchers in cooperation with another multigenerational cattle rancher. Our core beliefs include treating our beefalo humanely, raising our beefalo naturally and making sure our beefalo have space to roam. These beliefs help us create the best quality meat as our final product. Our beefalo spend their days in open pastures with clean air, fresh water and the time to grow slowly and naturally. Our animals are not fed antibiotics and NEVER receive hormones.
